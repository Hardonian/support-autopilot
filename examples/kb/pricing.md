# JobForge Pricing

## Plans

### Starter
- $0/month
- 100 jobs/month
- 1 worker
- Community support

### Pro
- $49/month
- 10,000 jobs/month
- 5 workers
- Email support
- Custom job types

### Enterprise
- Custom pricing
- Unlimited jobs
- Unlimited workers
- Priority support
- SLA guarantee
- Dedicated infrastructure

## Overages

Jobs exceeding plan limits are charged at $0.01 per job.

## Billing

Billing is monthly based on peak concurrent job count.

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.
